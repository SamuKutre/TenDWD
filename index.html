10 Dimensional World Decor is an autonomous company that deals with construction work in various sectors.

Our motto is to help you to build your dream home in an easy way at your comfort and happiness, while going hand in hand with sustainability.

  • 10DWD has its reach in all facets of construction and designing such as commercial, residential, social, etc.
  • Our vision is to give you a helping hand while maintaining the emotional connect in creating your dream home.
  • 10DWD also aims to change the image of owning a home in society today to an affordable and sustainable reality.
  • Additionally, 10DWD works at the social level to help the underprivileged get affordable and sturdy homes, schools and play areas.

About the CEO

Mayuri Gosewade

Overview

I.D. Mayuri Gosewade is an entrepreneur working in the field of freelancing and interior design for 5+ years.

Experience

She has worked with prominent interior designers in a freelancing capacity to aid with blueprinting, ideation and design.

Social Work

She is a recognized social worker and CEO of the NGO Sahyadri Foundation, which works with underprivileged sectors of society. Additionally, she is a founder of URS, which aims for establishing a clean India by reusing and recycling product, and also of LifeFirst which has successfully provided emergency services during covid crisis.
